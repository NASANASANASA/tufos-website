MEMBERSHIP

Join the Society

We sincerely welcome you to join the Taiwan UFO Society

We are Taiwan's only government-registered UFO research organization. Anyone who supports our mission can join as a full member. Founded in 1993 by a group of UFO research enthusiasts, the society was renamed the Taiwan UFO Research Society in March 2000 and became the Taiwan UFO Society in 2003. As a legally registered, non-profit social organization, our mission is to apply scientific methods to the study of UFOs (unidentified flying objects), prehistoric civilizations, paranormal abilities, life sciences, cosmic science, and related unexplained phenomena. We are currently Taiwan's only UFO research organization, regularly hosting lectures, exhibitions, and seminars, and publishing the "UFO Discovery" magazine for members, libraries, and schools nationwide.
